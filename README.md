## üßπ Data Cleaning & Preparation
To ensure high data integrity, the following steps were performed:
1.  **Integrity Checks:** Removed duplicates and handled missing values.
2.  **Standardization:** Corrected inconsistent entries (e.g., date formats and category naming).
3.  **Data Enrichment:** Created new calculated fields including `Month`, `Year`, and `Age Bracket`.
4.  **Table Structuring:** Converted the dataset into a **Structured Excel Table** for dynamic pivot operations.

---

## üìä Dashboard Features & Visuals
The interactive dashboard utilizes various charts to tell a story:
* **Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart):** Visualizes performance peaks and dips throughout the year.
* **Regional & Gender Analysis (Bar Charts):** Compares sales across different demographics and locations.
* **Top 5 States (Column Chart):** Highlights the geographic regions contributing most to revenue.
* **Customer Segmentation (Pie Chart):** Breaks down the buyer profile by age and category.
* **Interactive Slicers:** Allows users to filter the entire dashboard by month, channel, and category.

---

## üîç Key Insights
* **Peak Season:** Sales peak between **February and April**, followed by a decline, suggesting a strong seasonal dependency.
* **The Power of Women:** Female customers dominate the store's revenue, accounting for **64% of total sales**.
* **Demographic Sweet Spot:** Adult and Senior women contribute to over **50% of total orders**.
* **Top Locations:** Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the leading states.
* **Channel Performance:** Amazon and Myntra are the highest-performing retail channels.
* **Logistics:** A strong **93% delivery success rate**, though a 7% return rate suggests room for improvement in sizing/quality.
